Authentic French Café

Café Vendôme is an authentic French pastry, bakery and café located in Buckhead and City Springs.

Café Vendôme brings a pure, traditional atmosphere of the Parisian patisserie to Atlanta's community.

Café Vendôme uses ancestral artisinal techiques passed down from generation-to-generation to produce natural, delicious bread and pastries.

Café Vendôme is the perfect place for dates, working, studying, relaxing, or catching up with old friends!

Our Story

Café Vendôme was founded by our owner, Hamid. Hamid moved to Atlanta from the French Riviera. Hamid missed the homely, aromatic atmosphere of the cafés from his homeland.

Café Vendôme is a perfect embodiment of the French café. No detail goes un-noticed.

Café Vendôme uses state-of-the-art bakery equipment brought all the way from France.

All the pastries and baguettes are baked on-site all day long using carefully selected ingredients.

Our Menu

Café Vendôme is open for breakfast, brunch, and lunch.

Café Vendôme offers exquisite pastries, from nutella crepes, éclairs, fruit tarts, and macarons. A customer favourite is the mouth-watering chocolate almond croissant!

Café Vendôme's offers aromatic coffee, tea, and the freshest of natural fruit juice. One sip of our café au lait and you'll be in heaven!

For those with a bit of hunger, Café Vendôme offers savoury sandwiches, paninis, and salads. A customer favourite is the tasty croque monsieur!
